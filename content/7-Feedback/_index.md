Below is my personal feedback regarding my experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program, with the desire to help the FCAJ team improve on any shortcomings:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCAJ members are always ready to support me when I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me focus better. However, I think adding a few more networking or team bonding sessions would help everyone understand each other better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides very detailed guidance, explains clearly when I don't understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The team admin supports with procedures and documents, creating favorable conditions for me to work smoothly. I highly appreciate that the mentor allows me to try and solve problems on my own instead of just giving the answers.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major**  
The tasks assigned to me align with the knowledge I acquired at school, while also expanding into new areas that I had not previously been exposed to. As a result, I was able to consolidate my foundational knowledge while learning practical skills.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**  
During my internship, I learned many new skills, such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared a lot of practical experience that helped me better navigate my career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, working seriously but still having fun. When there is an urgent project, everyone makes an effort and supports each other regardless of their position. This makes me feel like a true part of the team, even though I am just an intern.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when necessary. In addition, being able to participate in internal training sessions is a huge plus.


### Other Questions
*   **What are you most satisfied with during your internship:** The detailed guidance from my mentor and being allowed to try solving problems myself instead of just receiving ready-made answers. 
*   **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns:** There should be more networking or team bonding sessions so everyone can understand each other better. 
*   **If recommending to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?:** Definitely yes, because the work is not only aligned with our majors to strengthen foundational knowledge but also helps us learn practical skills and provides excellent career orientation. 


### Suggestions & Expectations
*   **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?:** Enhance networking activities or team bonding to increase team cohesion. 
*   **Would you like to continue with this program in the future?:** Yes, the company environment is very positive, and the teamwork is excellent.
*   **Other comments (feel free to share):** I am very grateful to the FCAJ members for their support regardless of position, making me feel like a true part of the collective.
